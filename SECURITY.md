## Overview
This repository serves as a comprehensive collection of my networking research, CCNA preparation, and technical labs. Every configuration and script here is a product of my dedicated study and passion for building secure and efficient networks.

These labs are primarily designed and tested within the following environments:

- **Cisco IOS/IOSv** (Various versions)
- **Virtualization:** VMware Workstation Pro
